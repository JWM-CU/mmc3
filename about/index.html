About


iLab Link for Reservations

The Medicine Microscopy Core (MMC) is a confocal and wide field microscopy core available by reservation through the iLab system. We are part of the Columbia Center for Human Development, the Columbia Center for Stem Cell Therapies and the Department of Medicine and provide training, maintenance, and assitance with all instruments. We specialize in tissue scanning and high resolution multi-channel fluorescence imaging and welcome collaborations for custom image and analysis.

We are located in the 8th Floor of the William Black Building
